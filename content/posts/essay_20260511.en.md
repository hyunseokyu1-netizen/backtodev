It's been 7 weeks since I started this blog and started working with AI.

My first simple WiFi QR code site turned out so beautifully that it reminded me of my first experience with WordPress.

Then I created this blog. No DB, just GitHub so I could manage it later.

---]

## What I made in 7 weeks

I've made quite a few things in that time.

- Cassette Tape App - I wanted to listen to old MP3 files with the feel of tape. I even tried to get it listed on the store, but it finally worked.
- A game for the blind - I had this idea while camping. I wrote it overnight and it's in the store now.
- YouTube Autoplay Player - I made this because my kids kept asking me to change the video while driving. I uploaded it to GitHub because YouTube's policy might not allow it to be registered in the store.
- JobRadar - The resume management platform I wanted to build from the ground up. I even added AI cover letter writing to the login.
- Small Business Coupon App - An app that stamps coupons for places like cafes and vape shops. I've been thinking about it for a while, but I made an MVP first.

While building these, I learned a lot about AI, skills, agents, hooks, and MCP, which eventually led me to AI engineering.

## FOMO

When I first started using AI, it was fun. It was fun to build the features I needed and customize them as I went along.

But as I learned more and more, I started to feel like I should do it because everyone else is doing it, not because I need it.

There's a saying.

> The world is made by smart people who make it difficult for ordinary people to enter their realm. If you're frustrated, you should be the one to change that.

I didn't want to code AI for its own sake. I wanted to be someone who could create a business model using AI.

So why am I focusing on AI development?

My current state is **FOMO**.

> **FOMO** (Fear Of Missing Out) - "A state of anxiety that everyone else is doing it, but I'm not." The fear of missing out on an opportunity.

---

## I need to get organized

I have a lot of things I want to build, a lot of things I want to try. But I feel like I've gotten disorganized.

I need to get organized and focus on the things that make business sense.
