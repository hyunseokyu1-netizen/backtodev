With the news that Fable5 is switching to a pay-as-you-go model this Sunday...

I’ve really been using it like crazy all week. 

At least I’m making good use of the three 7-day passes I got last time. 

At first, I was just switching back and forth between my existing account and one new one...

But then I suddenly started working on games I’d always wanted to make, 

platforms, communities, and so on......

one thing led to another... so this week alone, I’ve created two new apps and two new platforms. 

On top of that, I’m still building up one platform I was already working on... but it’s stuck in the payment gateway approval process...

I started one or two projects because I didn’t want to waste my Fable5 time... but now I’m constantly switching between four accounts, 

moving from one to another whenever my tokens run out, and setting up separate login accounts for each project...

It’s a frantic cycle.

But even this is almost at its Fable limit... so it’s coming to an end.
