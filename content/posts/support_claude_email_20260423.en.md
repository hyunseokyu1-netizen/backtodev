I accidentally deleted my organization from the Claude console. And just like that, I was locked out — couldn't log in at all.

I went to the support page and tried the chat — but it just kept giving me automated AI responses. I needed to send an email, but there was no email address anywhere on the page.

Turns out, **you have to create a ticket through the chatbot first** before an email form becomes available. There's a specific flow you need to follow.

---

## The Flow

```
Log out → Open chatbot on support page → Select "I can't login" → Enter email → Ticket created → Write your email
```

---

## Step 1 — Log Out, Then Open the Chatbot on the Support Page

When you're logged in, the ticket creation option may not appear. **Log out first**, then visit the support page.

Click the chat icon to open the chatbot window.

![Open chatbot on support page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-57_006_1776906238561.jpg)

Once the chatbot window opens, send a message to start the conversation.

![Start chatbot conversation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-57_007_1776906279068.jpg)

---

## Step 2 — Select "I can't login"

You'll be presented with options. **You must select `I can't login`.**

![Select I can't login](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-58_008_1776906568646.jpg)

> If you click `Login now`, it just redirects you to the login page — an infinite loop when you can't log in to begin with.

---

## Step 3 — Accept Terms and Enter Your Email

Accept the terms when prompted.

![Accept terms](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-58_009_1776906583567.jpg)

Enter the email address you want to be contacted at.

![Enter email address](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-58_010_1776906592831.jpg)

---

## Step 4 — Open the Ticket and Write Your Email

You'll see a confirmation that a ticket has been created. Click on the ticket to open the email form.

![Ticket created](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-57_003_1776906833996.jpg)

Write your message and submit. You'll see a confirmation screen like this.

![Email submitted](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyunseokyu1-netizen/backtodev/main/public/images/KakaoTalk_Photo_2026-04-23-09-52-57_002_1776906854053.jpg)

---

## Summary

| What to do | Why |
|---|---|
| Log out first | Ticket option may not show when logged in |
| Select `I can't login` | `Login now` only redirects to the login page |
| Enter your email accurately | The ticket confirmation link is sent to that address |

These days, AI chatbots handle the first line of customer support everywhere. To actually reach a person, you have to work through the chatbot to generate a ticket first. Once you know the path, it's straightforward.
